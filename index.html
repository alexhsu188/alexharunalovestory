Our Love Story Timeline

The best and most unforgettable love moment that happened in our love story

16 October 2017

Meet in Time Square

This was the first time we talked when we were waiting in line for exchange money. I was so surprised we could had a amused and relaxing conversation in English.

21 October 2017

The Frst Weekend in Ayala Mall

The first time, we hung out with our batchmates on the weekend. Went shopping and ate dinner. I remebered we were always together, cuz I have to make translation between English and Mandarin. I was so enjoying this job...haha

28 October 2017

Oslob & Bohol Island

The wonderful 2 days trip. In this trip, we started getting close. As a result, I held your hand first time while we were chasing the sea turtle. Although we didn't fall in love at that time, it might make my heart turned toward you gradually.

31 October 2017

Island Hopping & Driking

The most unforgettable thing was not island hopping but drinking. Riku, you and I went to bar for drinking after island hopping. I got drunk and vomited a lot and then I almost died at that time.....haha. So weak I was, but it was absolutely a special memory I haven't had before.

23 November 2017

Talking Overnight in the Bamboo House

We talked overnight until 3:40 am. Even if you already laid down on my chest, but you were so surprised why I touched your upper arm. I still remembered your surprising facial expression, it made me feel so embarrassed...笑笑

26 November 2017

Moalboad & Kawasan Fall

The first time, Dai Chan and Ari also joined our travel. Everyone jumped from the cliff in Kawasan Falls, so it was very crazy and fantastic memories. The only bad thing was we were always separated into different group. So unlucky we were.

3 December 2017

The Last Trip in Philippine

We went to Adventure Cafe, Temple of Leah, Tops Lookout and Lantaw restaurant. This was my the last trip with you in the Philippine. We took many funny and creative photos in front of breathtaking sunset and nightview. I thought I already fascinated with you deeply and didn't wanna leave Fella 2.

9 December 2017

Leaving Day From Fella 2

Before I left, we hadn't slept for 2 days . We could share and talk anything and never felt tired at that time. Finally, I got our first kiss at the last night in Lobby. I will never forget that perfect memont. Although I didn't fall in love at first sight with you, at that moment, I already couldn't stop loving you.

6-8 January 2018

The First Time Meet in Taiwan

We went to a lot of places in Taiwan. The most special event was we successfully released the sky lantern in Pingxi. And although every one knew you were in Taiwan, we could still get much time to get together surreptitiously.

25 January 2018

The Second Time Meet in Taiwan

I tried to give you a surprise, but I was so stupid for taking wrong train to Taichung. Fortunately, I still arrived airport in time and saw the happiness on your face.     \(^0^)/

26-31 January 2018

Sun Moon Lake and Many Attractions

This time we had a lot of time could get together, so we went to Sun Moon Lake, Taipei City Zoo and batting center. I really enjoyed every moment with you, it didn't matter wherever we go.
