Delicious Breakfast in HsinChu

There are many great traditional Taiwanese food in HsinChu city. We will stop by these stores for breakfast on the way to amusement park.

Sesame Flat Bread House

Well known for Chinese sesame flat bread, egg pancake and soybean milk

Obasan Noodle Breakfast Shop

Fried bread stick in Chinese sesame flat bread and spicy wontons

水哥養生早餐

A variety of Rice Balls

Stanley's Steakhouse

The most recommendable dish is 16oz Grilled Thickly Steak. Most people left a great review for their perfect cooking and service. I can't wait to eat that with you.
