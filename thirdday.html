4/29 - Beef Noodle & NTU Tour

Mu Ji Beef Noodle

This place is very well known for its beef noodle, beef scallion roll, and shredded pork with Beijing sauce. The Tendon and beef noodle is unique. The tendon is silky smooth with a fairly neutral flavour.

National Taiwan University (NTU)

National Taiwan University is the most prestigious university in Taiwan. It was founded in 1928 during Japanese rule. It is a great place to take a stroll and I will be a student ambassadors to introduce our peaceful and beautiful campus.

金冠雞腿飯

We ate this last time in Taiwan. I believe you still miss it, so let's eat it again!!
